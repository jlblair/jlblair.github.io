Biography

With a Bachelor's degree from the University of Gerogia and $700 dollars to his name, Justin Blair ventured onward to study law at Georgia State University.

Interestingly enough, Justin was never considered a book worm; he played basketball competitively all throughout his life. He took it upon himself to be the one to sacrifice his own hoop dreams for the greater good of all of his friends and family.

Key connections and opportunities along the way enabled him to make a seemless transition from the study of law to the sports industry.

God's plan began to unfold for his life, and he realized more and more each day that he would become a big-time sports agent.

As some of his childhood friends and former teammates began to attract interest from professional scouts of playing sports professionally, he realized the unique opportunity he had to represent his friends in their athletic endeavors.
